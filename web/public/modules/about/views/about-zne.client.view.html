In order to determine the target electricity consumption of each of our four buildings, we triangulated four sources of data:

(1) West Village developer’s modeling of estimated consumption and PV production for each building

(2) Monthly utility bills reporting net consumption (consumption – production)

(3) Online solar production calculator to estimate monthly proportions of production relative to annual estimated PV production

(4) Baseline data from HOBO data loggers in each building. We excluded WCEC labs from 215 Sage ZNE goals and we excluded EV charging from ZNE goals for 1590 Tilia; both were added after initial ZNE planning.
